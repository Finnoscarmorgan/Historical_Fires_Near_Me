MELBOURNE. Friday. . Another lad named Cooper died yesterday from injuries received in the powder explosion. This makes the seventh death from this cause. Disastrous bushfires have broken out in the ' pastoral district between Belfast and Hawksdale, destroying a large quantify of fencing and grass ' as well on several homesteads. In some places mobs of sheep have been literally roasted alive. The fires raged over an area 5 miles wide and 14 miles long.
