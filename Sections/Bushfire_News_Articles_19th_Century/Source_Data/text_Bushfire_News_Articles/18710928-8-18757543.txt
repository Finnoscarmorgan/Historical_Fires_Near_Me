BARWES HIVER. An extract from a letter, dated Dungalear, Barwin River, September 20, states -" The hush fires have traversed a greater extent of country than I expected I am told they swept all the country in this locality, and right down to the Narran, and all the country on the back creeks has suffered I am happy to inform you it commenced rumng lost night, and rams steadily this morning, which ia a great blessing, comuig as it does nt the present to give the grass a chance to grow before the hot weather sets in.'*
