During the recent westerly gales a ' bush fire occurred on the Cobreei Hills, nearDungog. Although the fire pre sented a very imposing spectacle at .night, no further damage appears to have peen occasioned beyond the de struction of grass. This is early for bush-fires, but if the present weather continues, fires are bound to occur, and residents would do well to be prepared. On Saturday, the 22nd ultimo, a rifle match took place between the Lambton and Morpeth volunteer brigades, re sulting in a win for the latter by 12 points.
