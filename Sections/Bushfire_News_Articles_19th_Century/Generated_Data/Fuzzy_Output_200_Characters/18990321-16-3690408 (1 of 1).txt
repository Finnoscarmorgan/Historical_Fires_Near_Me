VICTORIA. (By Telegraph from Our Correspondent.) MELBOURNE, MARCH 20. DEATH SENTENCES COMMUTED. The Executive Council to-day considered the death sentences passed on Olga Rad- alyski and Travice Alexander Todd, who were convicted of the murder of Mabel Ambrose, the victim of the Yarra tragedy. The sentence on Radalyski was commuted to ten years' penal servitude, and on Todd to six years' penal servitude. HEAVY AND GENERAL RAINS. Heavy rain has been general throughout the colony since Saturday, and bushfires, which were raging in Gippsland, have been extinguished. Snow has fallen in some of the mountain districts. The temperature, which during nearly all last week was about 100deg. in the shade at Melbourne, is now less than 80deg.
