Published Every Friday. FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 6, 1885 At the time of going to press bush fires are raging extensively along at Arthur's Creek and at Kinglake. At the latter place, one of the settlers, Mr Pierce Power, has had a large portion of his outbuildings burnt down, just managing to keep his homestead.
