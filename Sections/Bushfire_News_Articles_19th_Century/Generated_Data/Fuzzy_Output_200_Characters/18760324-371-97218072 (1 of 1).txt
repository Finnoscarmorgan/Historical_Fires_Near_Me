Original Poetry. THE HILLS OF AUSTRALIA. Oh! tall and barren rising With rugged rocks and high, While bush-fires burn thy verdure And kings of forests die. All burnt, and bare, and ragged, Ah! where is nature's grace? There are the ugly barren hills, But of verdure not a trace.
