Pastoralistsand selectors have been sending their fat sheep to Sydney, where excellent prices have been obtained during the past few weeks. If rain does not fall shortly crops will be at least & partial failure, and grass Trill be scarce in summer. Shearing is progressing smoothly, and a number of eheds are nearly cut oat.
