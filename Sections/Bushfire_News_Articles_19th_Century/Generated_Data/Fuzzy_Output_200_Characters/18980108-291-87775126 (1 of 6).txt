TASMANIA. TREMENDOUS BUSH FIRES. MANY BUILDlNGS DESTROYED. SERIOUS LOSS OF LIFE. Hobart December 31. Bush-fires are raging in all directions. Mr. Henry Dobson's suburban residence, on Huon-road, with furniture and effects, was burnt to the ground to-day. About a hundred sailors from the warships in the port were sent out with a view of rendering assistance. The Longley Hotel was also totally destroyed. Several persons were seriously injured at Port Arthur, where some old penal establishments were burnt to the ground.
