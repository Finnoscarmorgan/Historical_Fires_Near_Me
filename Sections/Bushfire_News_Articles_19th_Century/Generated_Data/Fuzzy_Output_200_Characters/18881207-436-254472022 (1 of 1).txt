The nominations for Mulwala Handicap, Hurdle Race, and Publicans Purse are, I believe, to be in the hands of the secretary on or before the 17th December, at 8 pm. We were visited by a terrific dust-storm on Tuesday evening, which did an amount of damage in the way of unroofing houses and uprooting and tearing branches from trees. I have not heard of any accidents further than those mentioned, but I believe some persons in a trap had a narrow escape from a falling tree down at the punt. Several bush-fires are ragiog in this locality, and the air is quite full of smoke from them. ___
