An Agitator's Career. Among the sufferers by the late Vic- torian bush fires is David A. Andrade, who first introduced Anarchism into Australia. In 1885 Andrade, who is a Victorian native, casually came across some copies of Boston Liberty, organ of the Benjamin R. Tucker school of Anarchism, and became impressed with the ideas.
