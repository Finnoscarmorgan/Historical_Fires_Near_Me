Some two months ago, a little over a cwt. of stone from Mulga Mulga, was sent to Melbourne by Mr. Spalding, our popular Traffic Manager, for assay. This genial gentleman, has just been informed of the exceedingly satisfactory result of the test. It appears that the stone went 4oz 4dwts, 22grs. of gold and 1oz. 10dwts. of silver per ton. The Mulga Mulga Company have been, for some time past, trying to float a company to work their claims, but without success.
