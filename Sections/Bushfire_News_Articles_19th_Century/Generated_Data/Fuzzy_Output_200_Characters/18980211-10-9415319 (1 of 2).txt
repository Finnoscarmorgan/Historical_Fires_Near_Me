BUSH FIRES AT PORT ESPERANCE. THREE SAWMILLS BURNT. Terrific bush fires were raging here on the afternoon of Tuesday, 8th inst., which, before a hot N.W. wind, took everything in their course. Hopetoun sawmills (Innes') were burn t to the ground, leaving nothing standing but the chimney.
