WOLLOMBI. Little or nothing worth communicating has occurred here of late. Weather continues dry and excessively hot. At Howe's Valley there have been extensive bush-fires, burning up all the feed. Trade here rather dull, and money scarce. I have just received a report of a woman, the mother of a large family residing somewhere down the Cock- fighter, being burnt to death from her clothes acci- dentally taking fire, whilst washing. Name and other particulars at present unknown to me.
