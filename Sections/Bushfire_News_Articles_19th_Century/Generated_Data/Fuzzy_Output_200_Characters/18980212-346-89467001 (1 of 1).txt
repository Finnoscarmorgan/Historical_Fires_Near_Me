THE BUSH FIRES. (From the "Argus.") Foster, 10th February. Although there are still fires burning in the district no fears are entertained for the safety of the rest of Foster; provided ordinary pre- cautions are taken, the greater portion of the scrub having been thoroughly burnt out yester- day and today. Business is partly suspended, and selectors are still coming in to swell the numbers who have already been housed by the Foster residents.
