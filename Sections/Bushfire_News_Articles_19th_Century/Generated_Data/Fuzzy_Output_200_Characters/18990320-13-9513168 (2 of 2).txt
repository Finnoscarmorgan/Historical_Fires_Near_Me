BUSH FIRES IN GIPPSLAND GREAT DESTRUCTION OF PROPERTY. A NUMBER OF SETTLERS BURNT OUT. NEERIM. SOUTH, Saturday. Numerous bush fires were burning in many parts of the district last Thursday. The most destructive one visited Neerim North. Mr. Henry Beamish was com- pletely burnt out. His dwelling house which was a large one, and almost new, as well as all his outbuildings, were whollv swept away. With the high wind that was blowing it was found impossible to do any thing to stop the flames. Mr. Beamish is a heavy loser, all his grass and fences being burnt.
