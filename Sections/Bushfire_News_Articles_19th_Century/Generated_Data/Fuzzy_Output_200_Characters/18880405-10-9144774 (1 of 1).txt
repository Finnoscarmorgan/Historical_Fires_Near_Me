An inquest was held at Muddy Plains to- day on the body of John James Wing, who had dropped dead suddenly on Monday. Evidence showed he was found dead on the road by his son after great exertion in boating out a bushfire. The medical evidence showed he had weakness of the heart, and had Bun. cumbed to synoope. A verdict of death from natural causes was returned.
