The rain will prove a great boon to the farmers, many of whom were terribly short of water. CURRAMULKA, March 4th. The heat has been intense during the last few days, and yesterday an enormous bush-fire was raging between here and Maitland, the black smoke clouds sometimes completely obscuring the sun. Another fire was blazing away to the southward, near Port Vincent, and a smaller one to the east. Altogether, Curramulka has had a fair share of bush-fires this season. We have hardly had any rain during this summer, with the exception of one showery day a couple of weeks ago, and the soil for fallowing purposes is therefore very hard.
