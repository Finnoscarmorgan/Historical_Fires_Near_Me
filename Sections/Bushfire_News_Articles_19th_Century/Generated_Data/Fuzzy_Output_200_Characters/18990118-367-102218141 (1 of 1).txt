MOSS VALE. Bush-Fires are raging around town,. and have been burning with more or less severity during the past week. Several farmers around Manchester Square have had all their grass de- stroyed, and in many instances great difficulty has been experienced in saving tho dwellings and haystackes. Fresh fires broke out on Monday around Meryla Siding and the Green Hill, and they are still raging. A striking illustration of how these fires may originate was witnessed on Mon day .evening, when a spark from an engine passing through the town ignited the grass on the railway em bankment.
