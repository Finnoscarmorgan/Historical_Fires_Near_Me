SERIOUS BUSH FIRES. AVOCA, Thursday. —Age. Tremendous bush fires are raging here. One broke out at Green Hill Creek on Tues day afternoon, and spread with great rapidity, destroying several hay stacks and miles of fencing. Last evening it became so dangerous, and was travelling so rapidly towards Avoca, that a messenger was de spatched to town by those at work to raise the alarm. The firebell wa6 rung, and a large number of people immediately set out to render assistance. The fire was travelling with astonishing rapidity in some places, owing to the wind that was blowing.
