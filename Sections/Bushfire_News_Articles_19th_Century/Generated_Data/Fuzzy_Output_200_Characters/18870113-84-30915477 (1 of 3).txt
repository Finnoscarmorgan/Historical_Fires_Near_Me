GREAT liUSH FIRES IN VICO TORIA. TOcutMVAL, Sunday night.-In this part of the Riverina disastrous bush-fires make their appearance annually as regu larly as Christmas. In 1884 a serious fire played sad havoc amongst the settlers. It commenced on the 6th January, and every year since then a fire has broken out within 12 miles of that township, and every year it has been worse. Last year the lire was at its worst, when, providen tially, a heavy shower fell, and it was ex tinguished as suddenly as it began.
