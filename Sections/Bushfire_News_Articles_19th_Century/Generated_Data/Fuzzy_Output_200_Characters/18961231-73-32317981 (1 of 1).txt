In the meantime Bueban managed to extricate' himself from his manacles, and having re leased his comrades they bound the soldiers, withdrew the flints from their fireloeks and fled, taking a keg of the precious fluid with them. The soldiers managed to stagger back to the settlement and excused them selves the best way they could. Buchan and his mates were never afterwards heard of. and it was supposed that they perished in a devouring bushfire that neatly overwhelmed the soldiers as they trotted with long faces to the settlement.
