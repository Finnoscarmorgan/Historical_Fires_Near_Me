In the Daylesford district stock are reported as being fast reduced to living skeletons, and sheep are unsale- able at 1s. to 1s. 6d. per head. Residents at Lin- ton are put to great straits through the difficulty in procuring water for either domestic purposes, or for drinking. As much as 12s. per load of 100 gallons has been given for water obtained from the Flag- staff-spring. In the neighbourhood of Kyneton, Sandhurst, Ballarat, Lancefield, and other towns, bush-fires have been raging for several days, causing much loss and inconvenience."
