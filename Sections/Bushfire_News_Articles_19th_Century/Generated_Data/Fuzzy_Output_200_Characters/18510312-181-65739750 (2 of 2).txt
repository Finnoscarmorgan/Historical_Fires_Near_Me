In most countries, the greater tho risk, the more numerous are the facilities for insuring against it. I have no desire to dictate, but will merely hint that district societies might be formed to protect subscribing sufferers from casualties occasionsd by bush-fires. Should this important matter obtain notice from this simple view of the case, my end is answered ; otherwise, at some future time, I may endeavour to lay before the public my own ideas for their consideration.
