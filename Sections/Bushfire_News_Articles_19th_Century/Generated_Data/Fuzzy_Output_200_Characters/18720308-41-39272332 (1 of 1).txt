For more than a week bushfires have been blazing in all directions, and last night the heavens were red with the glare of a large one, supposed to be on the Coorong; but should the present wind continue,' the flames, which as yet have been chiefly confined to scrub, may prove disastrous. ? _ KINGSTON, March %.
