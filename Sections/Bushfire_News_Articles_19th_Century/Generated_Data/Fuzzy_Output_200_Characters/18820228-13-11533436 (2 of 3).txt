HORSHAM, MONDAY. The weather is beautifully fine and cool. MANSFIELD, MONDAY. Tho weather broke at 5 p.m. yesterday, after a storm of wind and dust. Thunder and lightning followed, and then rain set in, and has continued at intervals up to the present. Though the fall was not heavy, it will do an incalculable amount of good.
