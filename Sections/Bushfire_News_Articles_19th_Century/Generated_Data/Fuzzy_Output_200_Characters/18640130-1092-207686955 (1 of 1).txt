KAPUNDA. [Correspondent.] January 29. The bush-fire I * mentioned in my last was not on the Julia Creek, as stated, but in^the neighbourhood of Mr. Abraham White's, and Mr. G-illow's, on the Light. A.n inquest was held yesterday at the latter gentleman's residence, by Mr. J. S. Brown, S.M., Coroner, and a respectable jury, of whom Mr. O. White was foreman, to enquire into its origin. After a great many witnesses had been examined, the Jury returned a verdict to the effect, " that how the fire originated was unknown."
