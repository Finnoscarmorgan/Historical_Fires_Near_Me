GENERAL NEWS. The heat was intense at Grenfell on Christmas Day, and bush-fires aronnd were excessive. The Maryborough Chronicle reports that a little girl between five and six years of age, named Inker, lately strayed away from her home at Tiaro, and got lost in the bush. A search for her was made for six days, when she was found about two miles from home, very faint from want of food and exposure, but otherwise not much the worse.
