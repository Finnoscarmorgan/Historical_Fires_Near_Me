A YOUNG WOMAN ROASTED ALIVE NEAR NORTHAMPTON. From a gentleman who received a tele- gram from Northampton, yesterday, we learn that an accident of an appalling cha- racter occurred at a place called Willow Gully, near that township, on Sunday last. From the brief particulars to hand it appears that the extreme heat of the day was the cause of a bush-fire breaking out in a paddock near the homestead of Mr. John Williams, who, together with some of his family and neighbours, was speedily on the spot and worked, hard for a consider- able time in extinguishing the flames. A daughter of Mr. Williams, named Lizzie, observing the great fatigue of those engag- ed with the fire, set to work and made some tea for their refreshment, a supply of which she hurried off with to her father, during which, unfortunately, the flames caught her clothes.
