LIGHTNING A C AUSE OF BUSH FIRES. TO THE EDITOR OP THE AUSTRALASIAN, Sir,—As doubtless you are aware, nearly one - third of the grass lands in the Liverpool Plains around Walgett and Moree, towards the Queensland border, has been burnt during the last two or three months— the cause being lightning. On our place here, about 50 miles north of Moree, the lightning set the grass on fire on several occasions. The last time we were burnt com pletely out, together with some of our neigh bours.
