The weather has been fearfully hot the last three days, and yesterday was a regular brickfielder, everything literally buried with dust; two or three bush'fires were seen last night, but not of a very considerable extent; one seemed to be in or near Mr Dar lot's paddock, another between the township and Cherry Tree Hut, the third to the east of Brown's. To-day it is very pleasant, but cloudy, with a fine breeze from the south. 7 th December. MINING ACCIDENT.-Last Satur day in the shaft where the court house used to be, one of the men while below in the shaft got his leg broken by the fall of some of the rock.
