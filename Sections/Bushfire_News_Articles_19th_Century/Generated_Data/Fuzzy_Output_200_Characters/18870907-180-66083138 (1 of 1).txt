Master Frank Allnut met with a rather nasty accident the other week. -It appears he was breaking in a young horse, and while in the act of mounting the horse suddenly turned round and reared, striking him with his hoof upon the nose, severely injuring that organ, so that it had to be sewed up by the doctor.
