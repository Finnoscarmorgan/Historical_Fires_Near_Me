During the afternoon a south wind set in, bringing the smoke from distant bushfires, but also a cool change the thermometer falling about 20 degrees before .sunset. Rain threatened but only a few drops fell. During the night there was thunder at no great distance. To- day the atmosphere is clear, and the temperature cool and refreshing after the excessive heat.
