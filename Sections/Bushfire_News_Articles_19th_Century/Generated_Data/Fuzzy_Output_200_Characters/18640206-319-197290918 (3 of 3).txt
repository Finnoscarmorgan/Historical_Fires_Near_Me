Bush Fires.— Several bush fires raged about us on Sunday last. One in German Gully, near Gib raltar, was unusually fierce, and threatened at one time to spread far arid wide, but owing to the exer tions of the residents it was eventually extinguished. Beyond the Adelaide Lead there was also a similar visitation, which was not put out without consider able difficulty. The rank condition of the grass just now renders bush-fires of more than ordinary im portance. Too much caution cannot therefore be exercised by those who are in the habit of making outdoor fires. — Talbot Leader, February 2.
