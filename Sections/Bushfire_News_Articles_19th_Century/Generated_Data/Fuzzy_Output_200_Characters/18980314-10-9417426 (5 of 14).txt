It does seem strange that our governing authorities, with all their marked desire to spend money lavishly upon every useless trifle, should so grudge some forms of expenditure, that refusal was made to several solicitations year after year for the general publishing of this Bush Fire Act, or failing that, an official notice from the Attorney-General's office to be issued in December, calling brief attention to the rigid ruling of the Act, so that residents in the bush and elsewhere from town, might be duly informed and warned against an infraction of the law.
