TASMANIAN BUSHFIRES. DESTRUCTION OF LIFE AND PRO- PERTY. TRAGIC INCIDENT. Details of the bush fires which began on Mount Wellington, in Tasmania, on Friday, show that the devastation has been widespread, the flames rushing down the valleys towards the coast, sweeping miles of country, and destroying houses, orchards, and fences in its train. Several lives have been lost, the destruction of live stock also being heavy. The devas- tation on the Huon road, at Upper and Lower Longley, and at Sandfly, is deplor- able, dwellings, churches, post offices, hotels, farms, and dwellings being reduced to ashes.
