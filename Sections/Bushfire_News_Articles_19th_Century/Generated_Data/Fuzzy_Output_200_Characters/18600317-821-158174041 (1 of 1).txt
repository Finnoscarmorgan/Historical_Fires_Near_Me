The Ant did net reach this port till 10 a.m. on Thurs day, having laid in Lacepede Bay for the night. A great many settlers are at present in the township, the election business having drawn some, and o hers to see Mr. David Power off in the Ant to-day, that gentle man being about to leave this distiict to reside in Adelaide. The weather daring the past week has been very fine, although rather windy. SettlerB complain that the country is very dry, and that they want water very badly. The bush-fires have not all done for the season. One was burning within a few miles of the bay yesterday and last night.
