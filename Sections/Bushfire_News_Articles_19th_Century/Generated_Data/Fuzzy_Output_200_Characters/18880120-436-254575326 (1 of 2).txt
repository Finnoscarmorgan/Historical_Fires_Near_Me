ALBURY STOCK MARKET. ALBURY, Thursday. Messrs. CHAS. L. GRIFFITH and Co. report holding their usual fortnightly market at their Albury yards on Wednesday last, when they yarded 300 cattle and 1500 sheep. Owing to the numerous bush-fires in the district at present, the attendance was smaller than usual. Cattle.— 300 yarded. Best bullocks in (light weights) averaged £7, best cows (very prime and heavy) £7 10s ; 2 and 3-year-old locally-bred bullocks, in fresh condition, from £4 10s to £5 ; 2-yearold heifere, to £3 16s.
