What will long be remembered as Black Thursday was on February 6 in that eventful year. Then in May came the discovery of gold. It became difficult of course to get labour of any kind, and to secure any, employers wore obliged to give any wages that might be asked. I know as a fact that for two months Jones was paying all the money he received from government in wages alone. Hay and other forage ross 100 per cent., and was hard to be got. Much had been destroyed by bush-fires.
