IMMENSE BUSH-FIRE IN THE BERRIGAN DISTRICT- A NUMBER OF SQUATTERS BURNT OUT. TOCUMWAL, Wednesday. A bush fire, caused by the carelessness of a man who threw away a burning match after lighting his pipe, broke out yesterday in the Berrigan district on the selection of a farmer named Quade. A high wind from the north was blowing at the time, and the flame, spread with marvellous rapidity, causing immense destruction. In a short time, in spite of every exertion, the holdings of Quade, M'Lei lan, Cruickshank, Carter, Robinson, Aitken, Mitchell, Graham, Cameron, and others were more or less devastated by the flames.
