THE FIRE FIEND. Sherbrooke District Devastated : (From our Correspondent.) SHERBRROKE, Tuesday. Afer our efforts last week we were fondly hoping that the danger from the un- paralleled bush-fires that had been disfigur- ing the country for several days was quite over, but alas our sanguine anticipations have not been realised. Yesterday afternoon a strong breeze sprang up, and soon the bush below Mr. Dinning s property was a mass of flame. This was about half-past 4, and towards dusk the fire reached the Union Church, which was entirely demolished, only the organ and seats being saved.
