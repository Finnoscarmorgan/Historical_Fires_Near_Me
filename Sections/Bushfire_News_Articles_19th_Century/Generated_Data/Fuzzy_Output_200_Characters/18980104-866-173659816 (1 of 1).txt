THE RECENT BUSHFIRES. The Director of Education has received information that the Lower Longley school is not burnt down. Mr Rule says there is an old building further down the road which was formerly used as a school five years ago. He does not know whether this was burnt down or not. Mr Rule has had no news from the Mountain River, where the fire was raging. The Gordon school is burnt down. This school stands back in the bush.
