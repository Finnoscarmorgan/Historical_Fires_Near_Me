TAL MURDER NEAR BRIGHTON. Melbourne, March 15. A brutal murder has been committed near Brighton. An Englishman, named Oldham, had been drinking with several Irishmen ; after which a quarrel arose. Six Irishmen then kicked and beat their victim most brutally. He crawled away into the bush, and next morning crept back to his tent. His assailants then fell upon him again; and one jumped upon his body. He lingered till Jioon, when he died. Seven men are in custody. NEW SOUTH WALES. Sydney dates are to the 12th. The Herald contains an article suggesting subscriptions for the relief of the sufferers by the bush-fires in South Australia.
