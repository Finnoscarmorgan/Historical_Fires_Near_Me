DESTRUCTIVE BUSH FIRES. [BY TELEGRAPH.] (FROM OUR OWN CORRESPONDENTS .) INGLEWOOD, MONDAY. The weather has been very hot during the last three days. Several bush fires have been burning. A light shower of rain fell this evening, and there are indications of a change. KILMORE, MONDAY. Several bush fires have been raging in this district during last week, and yesterday one broke out in the hospital paddock, doing con- siderable damage to fences and grass. PORT ALBERT, MONDAY.
