A SHOCKING DEATH.-Mudgee, Monday.-A shock. ing occurrence has been reported to the police authorities. A shepherd named William Kennedy, who was in the employ of Mr Hugh Farrelly of Rylstone, whilst endeavouring to extinguish a bush fire, got into the thick undergrowth, and being surrounded by flames before he could make his escape, was literally roasted to death.--Telegram to S. M. Herald.
