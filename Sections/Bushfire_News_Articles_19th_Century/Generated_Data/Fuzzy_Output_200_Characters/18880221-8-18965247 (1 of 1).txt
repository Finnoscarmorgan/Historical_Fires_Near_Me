Pastoral News, Stock Movements and Markets. DUBBO-WEATHEB REPORT.-There has been an agreeable change in the weather. On Sunday it commenced raining, and continued throughout the night and next day. It rained at intervals on Mon- day night. About two inches of rain fell. The rain was wanted. It will put a stop to bush-fireB, and freshen up the feed.-Dispatch, Feb. 17.
