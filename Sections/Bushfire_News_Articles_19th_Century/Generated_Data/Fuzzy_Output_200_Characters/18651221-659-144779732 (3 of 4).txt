To the carelessness of shepherds in leaving the fire used to prepare their noon-day meal are the ruinous bush-fires of this season mainly at- tributable. If proof is necessary witness the burnt state of the country downwards from Tarcutta along the route sheep travelled up. Your obedient servant, A FARMER, Tumut, 15th December, 1855.
