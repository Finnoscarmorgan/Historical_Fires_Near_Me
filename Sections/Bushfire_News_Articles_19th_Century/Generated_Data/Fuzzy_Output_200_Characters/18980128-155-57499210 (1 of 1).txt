BICYCLE SPORTS AT ALEXANDRA. It was with fear and trembling that the committee of the Alexandra Bicycle Club saw old Sol rise through his halo of bush fire smoke last Wednesday, for was not the much-talked-of race meeting at hand, and their trust was that public opinion would recognise the attractions of a pro gramme on which such gigantic pains and no little expenditure (from an extremely low exchequer) had been expended to render of uncommon interest.
