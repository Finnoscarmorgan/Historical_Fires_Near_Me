THE BUSH FIRES. Murchison, Thursday week. The bush fires in the neighbourhood of Wahring, are still burning. Mr Thomas Cahill, of Wahring, has lost four stacks of wheat and oats, and reaping machine. Mr J. Kendy has had .acres of grass and over four miles of fencing burnt. Mr J. Breen, residing seven miles from Murchi son has lost over six miles of fencing and 700 acres of grass. Messrs J. Cahill and Robert Barrow, of Dargaland, have had a considerable, quantity of grass and fencing burnt. The wind having changed, it is not expected that the fires will extend much further.
