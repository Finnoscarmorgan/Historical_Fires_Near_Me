The house and land had been sold a few weeks previously, the previous owner, Mr. David Williamson, having removed to Port Albert with his wife and family only a few days ago. At present the weather is cloudy, and becoming cooler, with the wind from the south, but unless a fall of rain occurs this evening, of which there is some prospect, it is feared that further damage will be caused by the fires, the grass being everywhere abundant. ST. ARNAUD, MONDAY. A bush fire occurred at Gre Gre yesterday afternoon. It was caused by a local farmer engaged in clearing operations, and destroyed several miles of selectors' fences and many hundred acres of grass.
