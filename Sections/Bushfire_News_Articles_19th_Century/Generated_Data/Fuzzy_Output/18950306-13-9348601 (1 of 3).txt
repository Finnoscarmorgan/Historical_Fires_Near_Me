BUSH FIRES AT ROCHESTER. ROCHESTER, TUESDAY. This afternoon a bushfire broke out on the Rochester-park Estate. The smoke had been noticed for some time, but it was thought that somebody was burnlng- off rubbish on the outskirts of the town. Information, however, was brought in to town that a bush fire was raging in the property known as the Park, und Mr. Doherty, one of the proprietors, at once secured a party to proceed to the scene.
