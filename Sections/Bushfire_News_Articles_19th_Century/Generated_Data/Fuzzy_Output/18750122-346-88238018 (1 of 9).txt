THE WEATHER AND BUSH-FIRES. The heat yesterday was most intense, and from all quarters, as will be seen from what follows, we receive accounts of disasters that have been caused by bush fires. The city was throughout the day enveloped in dense clouds of smoke. The thermometer registered at Messrs. Joseph at Co.'s, Pall Mall, as follows: 9 a.m., 90°; noon, 101 ° ; 3 p.m., 102° ; 6 p.m., 99°; maximum, 101° ; minimum, 78 ° . Barometer - 9 a.m., 29.140; 6 p.m., 29.020.
