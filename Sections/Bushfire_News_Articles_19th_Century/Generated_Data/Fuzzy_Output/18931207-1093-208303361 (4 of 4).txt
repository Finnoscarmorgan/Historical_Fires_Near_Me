The cause of the fire is at present unknown. Milang, December 6. The fierce heat of yesterday started a terrific bush fire in the vicinity of Campbell Park, a run some twelve miles from Meningie. The flames rose to a great lieighb, as they were clearly visible from Milang. No details have come to hand concerning the damage done, but from the magnitude of the fire it must have been considerable. Melrose, December 4. A large bush fire has been burning at the back of Mount Remarkable for the last two days. No particulars are yet to hand.
