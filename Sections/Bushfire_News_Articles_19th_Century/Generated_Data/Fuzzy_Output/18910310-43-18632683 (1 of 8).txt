THE HEAT WAVE. DISASTROUS BUSH FIRES. THE intense heat experienced during the latter end of last week, resulted in the outbreak of numerous bushfires. One of the most disastrous of these was one which broke out in the Macarthur district on the Mount Napier Station. Miles of country were swept by the fire, and an enormous amount of damage is said to have been done. Another fire was also reported from Dunmore, whilst the country from Linton towards Mount Mercer, was also swept.
