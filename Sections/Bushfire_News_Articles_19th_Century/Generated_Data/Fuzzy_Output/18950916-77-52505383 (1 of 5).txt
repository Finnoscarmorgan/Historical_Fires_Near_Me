WINTON. [FROM OUR OWN CORRESPONDENT.] The weather for the last week has been very unsettled, heavy banks of clouds coming up from the east and hazy skies giving every evidence of extensive bush fires. They must, however, be a very long way off, as there is no glare observable in the sky at night. We are now beginning to want rain badly. Unless we get a thunderstorm or two during this and next month the stock on the neigh bouring stations are sure to suffer, as bush fires will certainly break out from time to time.
