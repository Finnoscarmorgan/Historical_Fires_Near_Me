River News. Bush-fires were breaking out along the river previous to the late rains, but they are now extinguished. A lecture on " The Soils of the Hakes bury River for Fruit-growing" was delivered at Mr. Pemberton's, Mangrove- creek, on Saturday, 19th ult., by Mr. Angus Mackay, F.C.S. There was a good attendance of river folk present. The bridge ever Craft's-creek, near Man grove, is at present in a bad state, and needs attending to. The removing of snags from Mangrove Creek, which have been causing the river to silt up, is being attended to.
