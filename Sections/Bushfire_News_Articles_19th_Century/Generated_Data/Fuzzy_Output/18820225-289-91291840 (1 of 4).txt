Bushfires in the South. Goolwa, February 22. Large bushfires between this place and Willunga have again burnt down telegraph poles, and for a time interrupted telegraphic communication with Adelaide, which at this particular time, when the Darling trade is opening and large quantities of goods and orders are coming forward for shipment, means serious loss, annoyance, and con fusion to all connected with the river.
