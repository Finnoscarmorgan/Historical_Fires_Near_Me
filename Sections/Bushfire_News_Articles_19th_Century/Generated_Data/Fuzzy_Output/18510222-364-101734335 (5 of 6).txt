Grey and Marr, adjoining Kennedy Brothers, have lost 3000 sheep by bush-fires, also Mr. Ritchie, near Mount Napier, has got all his crops destroyed \--his paddock fence, is likewise burned to the ground. The driver of the Portland mail says, that as he was passing the Bell Post Hill, on Thursday, he saw one of the largest mobs of horses he ever met with in the colony. The creatures were galloping with the wind in the direction of the Barrabool Hills. They numbered he says upwards of eight hundred, and he supposes they had been driven off their runs by the bush fires.
