HUMOROUS COLUMN. The Latest Joke. Pat, meeting a fellow-countryman in George-street, Sydney, the other day, exclaimed : ' Arrah, Mike, have ye heard about the war in the Transvaal ? It's raging Ioike a bush-fire.' 'Yes, Pat,' replied Mike, 'I smelt the smoke from ii lasht noight. Shure the Sydney papers are up to date wid information regarding it ; but it licke me how they git their news wid all the telegraph wires out down. 1 think they must use the mouth of the cannon for a telephone to spake through.' ' Perhaps they do, ' said Pat. ' But 1 did ye hear the latest ?' ' No, Mike ; phwat was it ?' 'The Australians slaughtered about a hundred Boera at Glebe Island last Monday.' 'You don't mane to eay sol How did they git to Glebe Island ?' ' Oh, aialy enough,' said Pat. ' I mane boar pifea, you know.'
