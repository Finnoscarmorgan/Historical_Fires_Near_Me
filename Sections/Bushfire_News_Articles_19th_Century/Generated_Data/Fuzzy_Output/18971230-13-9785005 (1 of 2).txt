BUSH FIRE AT YARRAM. YARRAM, Wednesday. During the last two days the heat has been intense, Monday being a record for the district. Large bush fires are raging on the hills, and arce doing considerable damage. A fire broke out at Wonwron, and has travelled over five miles, destroying fences and grass. Great damage has been done, and many farmers in that locality are busily employed in coping with the fire. Last night the town was enveloped in smoke, the heat being most oppressive.
