FLYNN'S CREEK. (FROM OUR OWN CORRESPONDENT.) March 27. An extensive bushfire took place in the Upper Flynn's Creek district a few days ago which, before being extinguished, destroyed a large quantity of grass and many chains of fencing. It ignited close to the State school, and owing to the hot weather, swept rapidly through the properties of Messrs Waters, Collins, Maxfield, Enticott, Portch and Norris. A host of energetic workers turned out to stay its progress, but for some time without effect ; in fact, it was the rain that eventually extinguished the flames.
