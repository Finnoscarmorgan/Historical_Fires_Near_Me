REVIEW. Fifty Years Ago ; an Australian Tale. By CHARLES De Boos. In fifteen monthly parts, 1471 pp. Pub- lished by, Gordon and Gotch, George-street, and printed by F. Cuninghame, of Pitt-street, Sydney. "Fifty Years Ago" is the name of a spirited Aus- tralian tale, lately produced by one of our colonial writers - a gentleman whose talents have, for many years past been deservedly appreciated in the community, where his vivid sketches of Bush Life and Scenery, dry, caustic humor, and familiar know- ledge of the numerous quaint peculiarities of the colonial character, (as permanently developed in the oldest settlement in this part of the world), have been keenly relished, both in the wilds of the interior and in our rising towns and half-formed villages.
