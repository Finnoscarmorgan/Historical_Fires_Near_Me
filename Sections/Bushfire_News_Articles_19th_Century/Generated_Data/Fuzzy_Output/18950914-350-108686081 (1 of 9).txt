Bush Fires. GREAT DAMAGE TO PROPERTY ON THE MOUNTAINS. The passengers by the midday train from Bathurst to Sydney on Tuesday had a sensational experience near Soldier's Pinch, between Blackheath and Mount "Victoria. The train had to pass through a fearful bush fire, which raged on both sides and across the line, and the men on the engine had the hair on their heads and faces Binged and their coats burned.
