TERRIFIC BUSH FIRE.-The fire which has been noticed for the last two nights in the South-East has proved to be much more destructive than, any one contemplated. "We are informed by a gentleman who came into town early on Monday morning that on Sun- day last the township of Macclesfield had a narrow escape from total destruction by means of it. Our informant tells us that between 9 and 11 o'clock Sunday last the flames were seen coming up the Adelaide road towards Macclesfield in a broad compact mass extending to a wide distance on both sides of the road and carrying everything before it.
