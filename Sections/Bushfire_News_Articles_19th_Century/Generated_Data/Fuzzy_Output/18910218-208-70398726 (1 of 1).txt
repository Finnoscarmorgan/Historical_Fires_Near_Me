FRANKSTON. (From our own Correspondent) At the beginning of last week an extensive bushfire took place here, which although bravely fought by the local fire brigade and other towns people did a great deal of damage. We had another visitation from the devouring element on Saturday at about two o'clock. It was first observed that the scrub and under growth about two miles from Frankston towards Carrum was on fire. A strong wind from the north was blowing at the time and the fire spread rapidly, both sides of the Melbourne road being on fire; also the railway line, where some chains of fencing were destroyed.
