At Ingliston, several thousand sheep, and pro- perty to the value of some thousands of pounds is reported to have been destroyed. From the Grange, a report has been received that on Friday the 21st ult.; 2000 of Mr. Moffatt's sheep have been destroyed by a bush-fire. Messrs. Grey and Marr, adjoining Kennedy Brothers, have lost 3000 sheep by bush-fires, also Mr. Ritchie, near Mount Napier, has got all his crops destroyed \--his paddock fence, is likewise burned to the ground. The driver of the Portland mail says, that as he was passing the Bell Post Hill, on Thursday, he saw one of the largest mobs of horses he ever met with in the colony. The creatures were galloping with the wind in the direction of the Barrabool Hills.
