Moreton Bay Free Press, Jan. l8. Brisbane.-The Weather -On Tuesday IabI, a terrific hot wind blew, all day, which was aggravated in intensity by the bush-fires, which raged to windward. The next day, a marked change arrived in the temperature, and Thursday closed with a shower. Friday and Saturday were more or less rainy, very much to the benefit of the agriculturists. -On Saturday evening, a thunderstorm, of short duration, broke over the town, leaving the atmosphere clear and cool. On Sunday a good deal of rain fell in successive showers, which ceased before Monday morning.-Moreton Bay Free Press, Jan. l8.
