SOUTHERN DISTRICTS (From tho Goulburn Papers of Saturday.) BUSH FIRES.-The inhabitants were gratifying them- selves about the beginning of the week that the sum- mer was breaking up, and that autumn was about set- ting in. However the last two days have served com- pletely to disappoint these amicipations, the weather having been excessively hot. To add to the exhaustive heat of the temperature, Goulburn appears to have been surrounded with desolating bush-fires.
