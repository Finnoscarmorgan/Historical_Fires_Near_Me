PENOLA, Match 2. ! We have had very warm weather in Penola for the last few weeks. Hot winds have been plentiful, and bush-fires of daily occurrence. So far we have cause for thankfulness in that no serious damage has been done by the fires. The forthcoming general elections are causing no stir here as yet.
