The Rosebrook homestead was attached on Sunday, but after a fierce fight with the flames it was driven baek. The fire has now reached the Wondah Station, owned by Mr. Mosman. The largest fire that has occurred in the Horsham district for many years broke out yesterday, and swept an immense tract of country, destroying thousands of acres of grass and many miles of fencing. Benalla reports alarming news regarding the bush fires in the neighborhood of Toouibullup and Tooma, and both fires reached Benalla yesterday afternoon. Several fires in the ranges, which have been burning for some weeks past, have been confined chiefly to the hilly and barren country, but, with the arrival of south winds, they have been carried into the Toombullup country, and it is estimated that the several fires, which have merged into one. extend over an area of fill miles.
