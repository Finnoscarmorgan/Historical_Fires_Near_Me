Kooweerup reports that the railway bridge is in danger. A terrible bush fire has broken out in the Merang district, about fifteen miles north-east of Melbourne. About twenty farmers are completely burnt out. The fire was started by a man burning some stubble. It is estimated that about 50,000 acres were swept by the flames. Merang is a fruit-growing district. The fire is still raging. February 11 A destructive bush fire is raging at Mansfield, and immense damage has been done. On one run about 3,000 sheep were roasted to death, while rabbits in thou- sands have been destroyed.
