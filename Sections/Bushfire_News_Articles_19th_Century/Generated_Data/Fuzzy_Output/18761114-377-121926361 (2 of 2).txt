If he is aware of the mischief which his thoughtlessness has occasioned, his con science must reproach him while he lives. It should ever be borne in mind that " careless ness is next to maliciousness." Everyone who uses a match should take care that the flame is blown out before it is thrown away. The general adoption of this simple expedient would save thousands of pounds in this colony every year by the prevention of bush fires-the aggregate losses by which amount in this district alone to a much larger sum than would readily be believed, unless by those who are cognisanut of them. And which is of far greater importance-it would also prevent such distressing accidents as those recently recorded. Let smokers, in particular, see to it, then, that the habit in which they indulge does not needlessly in crease risk to life and property.
