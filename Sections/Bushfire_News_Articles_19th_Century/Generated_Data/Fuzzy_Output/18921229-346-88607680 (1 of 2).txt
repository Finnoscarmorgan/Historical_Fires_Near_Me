BUSHFIRE AT GUNBOWER AND COHUNA Our Kerang correspondent in amplifying his telegraphic report of yesterday writes:- On Saturday last a bush fire of great magnitude broke out at Gunbower West, and extended to south Macorna and Cohuna, covering altogether fully 50 square miles of settled country. To give an idea,of this country it is necessary to state that it consists of an old box forest, with a few hundred acres of mallee.
