BUSH FIRES IN THE HEYTESBURY FOREST. Great Loss of Property. The residents of the western and south western portions of the colony will have cause to bear in remembrance the terrible experiences of Tuesday last. Fires ap- peared to be raging on every hand, and the darkness which prevailed for some hours, almost dense enough to be felt, added to the horror of the situation, as, while everyone expected the devouring element to swoop down on the settlements, no one could tell from which direction the fire would come.
