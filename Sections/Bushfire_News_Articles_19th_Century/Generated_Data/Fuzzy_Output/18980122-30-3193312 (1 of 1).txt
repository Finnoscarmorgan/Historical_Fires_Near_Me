THE WEATHER. SHADE TEMPERATURE OF 106'9 IN PERTH. Yesterday, with a hot wind blowing for the greater part of the day from the east, and with numerous evidences of bushfires in the surrounding hills, was the hottest day experienced this summer, and until the sea breeze set in after four o'clock there were few who did not find the temperature very trying.
