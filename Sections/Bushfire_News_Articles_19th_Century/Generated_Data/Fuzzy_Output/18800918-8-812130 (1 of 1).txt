DISTRICT NEWS. [From our Various Correspondents.] Woodville. " The earth is born again in beauty." We have been blessed with a most welcome fall of rain, and the hearts of man and beast are alike thankful. A bush fire broke out last Saturday week, at Loch Goyle, the property of Mrs. Young. A fresh easterly breeze was blowing at the time, and caused the flames to spread very rapidly. It soon made its way to Clifden, the adjoining estate; but as a great num- ber of willing hands were soon on the spot, and arrested its progress, the only material damage done was the destruction of some fencing. The loss of the grass burnt will not be felt after the late ruin.
