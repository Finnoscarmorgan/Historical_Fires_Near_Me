Bush Fires in Victoria. (Melbourne Argus, Jan. 6.) Buninyong, Tuesday.--At half-past four o'clock this afternoon, bush fires, principally from the south-west, caused the township to look quite Black Thursday like, the atmosphere being thick with smoke. Clunes, Tuesday.--A large tract of grass country at Birch's Creek, near Clunes, was destroyed by fire to-day. The flames attacked the farm of M'Callum Brothers, whose homestead was with difficulty saved. All the stacks were burned, re- presenting the entire farming operations of the year.
