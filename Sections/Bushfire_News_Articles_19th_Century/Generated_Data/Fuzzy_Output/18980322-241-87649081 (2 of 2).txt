All pas sengers and cargo have to be landed or loaded in a small boat as the deuth of water at the jetty is insufficient for the Casino, The residents are agitat ing for an extension into deeper water to enable the steamer to lie at the the jetty. A public- meeting will be held on the 22th inst. to consider this matter. The output of potatoes, onions and dairy produce will be much below the estimate owing to the bushfires having burnt all feed and damaged so many crops. This will affect the trade of the Casino, as she will not have the cargo to take to Melbourne. The tender of Mr A. Costin has been accepted for the erection of a building for the Rocket Life Saving apparatus.
