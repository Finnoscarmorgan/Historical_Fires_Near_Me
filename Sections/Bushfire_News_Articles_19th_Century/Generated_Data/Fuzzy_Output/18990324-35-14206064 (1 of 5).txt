BUSH FIRE AT HURSTVILLE. About noon yesterday a dangerous bush fire broke out at the rear of Mr. Peter Moore's bush at Bel- more. The flames spread so rapidly as to reach in a very short space of time a distance of fully four miles, covering from the commencing point Walker's bush, better known as Oatley's bush, thence through into Mr. H. Kinsela's bush on to the Croydon- road near Sharp-street, Canterbury, on the Hurst- ville side of Wolli Creek, taking in Taylor's bush in Canterbury.
