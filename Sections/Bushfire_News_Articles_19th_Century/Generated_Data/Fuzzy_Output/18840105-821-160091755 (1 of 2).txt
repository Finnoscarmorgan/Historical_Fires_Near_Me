The Wagga Wagga AdrertUer of December 22 writes:—"The weather during the past' week has been fine and warm, and, as was anticipated, several bush-fires have occurred in the district. There are no stock move ments of consequence to report. The coming festive week is not likely to enliven matters, as usually matters are quiet during Christ mas." The JJuhho Dispatch says:—" The weather during the past week has continued dry and exceptionally hot. Saturday, Sunday, and Monday were very warm, ana Tuesday was dull and it looked like rain. It, how ever, only looked that way, for the day passed without any falling. We want a good fall of rain just now, not only so far as Dubbo is concerned, but for other parts of the district.
