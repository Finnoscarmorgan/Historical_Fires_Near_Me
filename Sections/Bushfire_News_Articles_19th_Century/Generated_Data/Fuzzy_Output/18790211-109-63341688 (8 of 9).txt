Out Cheswick, Bungaree, and Smythsdale way the forest is on fire, and daily records of disaster reach Ballarat. News from Smythsdale states that the long-continued heat has dried up the waterholes and baked the fruit on the trees. In Ballarat there has been almost a counter- part of Black Thursday.
