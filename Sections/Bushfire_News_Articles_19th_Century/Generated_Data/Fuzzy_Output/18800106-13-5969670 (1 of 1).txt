Dawson was brought up at the City Police Court this morning, and remanded for a week, before the expiration of which time the police hope to capture the remainder of the gang. The charge preferred against the miner John Wearne, of having committed an indecent assault on a young woman named Mary Ann Wilcocks, at Happy Valley, on the 31st ult., has been settled by the marriage of the parties concerned. The weather to-day has been oppressively hot, and several bushfires are raging in the district surrounding the city.
