COUNTRY NEWS. EIGHT or nine families have been burned out by a bush fire in the Woodford Forest, near Portland. This is said- to be the largest fire that has occurred in that locality for the last eight years. A BOY named Trematic has been drowned while bathing in the Tullaroop Creek, above Clunes. A YOUNG man named Coultard, who with two others was capsized an a boat on the Thompson River, was drowned. ON Tuesday night, a miner, named David Ellis, was accidentally lulled while working in a drive on Christmas Reef, by the roof falling JO upon him.
