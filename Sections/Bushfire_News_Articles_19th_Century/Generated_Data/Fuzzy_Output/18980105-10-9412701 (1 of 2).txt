LOSSES AT HUONVILLE. Mr Charles Oates writes; - "I had destroyed by fire during Friday last—One saw mill, all complete ; cottage of two rooms, hut, blacksmith's shop and tools, timber waggon and wheels; a cow shed, to hold 20 cows, 60ft long by 40ft ; 100 apple trees in full bearing burnt, and apples roasted ; about 1½ mile tram- road and bridges, 14 chains 14 x 2 water troughing, and 1½ mile of fencing, one 6-stall stable (weatherboard). One cottage of 5 rooms caught fire several times, but we managed to save it. Also a house of 8 rooms was saved after many hours of labour.
