THE HEAT WAVE. Large Bush Fires. Some More Records. [BY TELEGRAPH.] SYDNEY, Wednesday. Though the heat in most parts of the colony had yesterday moderated, the weather was still generally warm ; but the experiences of the previous day overshadowed that comparatively small discomfort. Concerning Monday's heat, 93 country stations reported ; and of these no less than 64 reported tem- peratures ranging from 100 to 118 degrees. Many country townships now report destructive bush fires.
