Many country townships now report destructive bush fires. At Crookwell several unfortunate selectors have been burned out ; and along the Murrumbidgee a considerable quantity of wheat has been destroyed. At Candago numerous dwellings, dairies, and stock were burned. Several have had to take refuge on the Gantawanglo River. Over a hundred townspeople were engaged yesterday in saving the town- ship of Bombala from the ravages of the surrounding bushfires. Ten miles of fencing as well as the main bridge over the Deepwater Creek at Bungendore have been burned.
