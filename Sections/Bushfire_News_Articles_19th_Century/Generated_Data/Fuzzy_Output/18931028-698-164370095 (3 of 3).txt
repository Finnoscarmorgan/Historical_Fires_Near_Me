The chair was occupied by Mr. Thomas Colls, M.L.A.. and the vice-chair by Mr. W. F. Wilkinson. Mr. Cross will be greatly missed by the poor. The working men of the town intend to give him a send off. Political matters are beginning to liven up. There will be several candidates. Mr. Argyle M'CaUum, protectionist, inlands contesting the seat against Mr. Colls, who is also a protectionist. Mr. Affleck and Mr. Dan Leahy, both freetraders, will also come forward.
