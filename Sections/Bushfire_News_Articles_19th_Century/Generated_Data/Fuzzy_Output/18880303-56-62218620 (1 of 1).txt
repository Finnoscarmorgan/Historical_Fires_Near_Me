DUBBO. (Dispatch.) The Weather since our last has been fine. The country around looks splendid. The late rains freshened up the food, and put out some bush-fires which threatened to do much damage. The rain was generally through the north-west. Down the Castlereagh it was very heavy in some places. The fall was abnormally large at Gulargambone Stock of all descriptions are in good order.
