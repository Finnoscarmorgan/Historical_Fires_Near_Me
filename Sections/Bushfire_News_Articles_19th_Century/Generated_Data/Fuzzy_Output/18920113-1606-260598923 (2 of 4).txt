DESTRUCTIVE BUSH.FIRES. OUR Williums River correspondent writes : Lately the' bush fires have been very close and prevalent. There have been some narrow escapes and in several cases loss of property has been caused. Mr. M. Quinn, of Glenfield Farm, has bad his haystack and a considerable amount of fencing burnt. Mr. Hamersley, of Richmond, on Friday last was nearly burnt out and would have been but for the prompt and willing assistance rendered by all the settlers that could be gathered together, many of whom had been hard at similar work for previous nights.
