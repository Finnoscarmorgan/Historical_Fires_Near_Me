 Pennant Hills. Fires. — A very large bush-fire has been raging for several days at the back of Mr. W. Aiken's property toward Thornleigh. Cricket.— Our locals are plucking up their courge and look upon their prospects for the season as good. Among the latest members are old representatives of the Dural and Carling- ford clubs, as well as a couple of new arrivals in the district, reported to be good acquisitions to the club.
