ALARMING NEWS.—A gentleman lately ar- rived from Maneroo informs us, that a large bush-fire is raging on the Gipps Land side of the Snowy river, evidently making its way towards the pastures of the Maneroo country. Burnt leaves and pieces of bark have been picked up at a distance of thirty miles from the front line of the conflagration. The grass in this part of the country is very long and should the fire once take hold of it the most disastrous consequences must follow. —Goulburn Herald.
