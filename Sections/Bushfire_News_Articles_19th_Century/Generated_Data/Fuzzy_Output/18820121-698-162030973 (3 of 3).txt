The process of uprooting the Warren Ponds vines will be commenced immediately. A telegram has been received from the rock-clearing party at the Heads, intimating that another rock has been discovered in the fairway 25 feet below the sur face. A diver examined it, and found five pinnacles which require removing. The weather was scorching hot to- day, the most oppressive of the season. Reports from the country districts state that the heat is dreadful, and .bush fires are raging in some localities.
