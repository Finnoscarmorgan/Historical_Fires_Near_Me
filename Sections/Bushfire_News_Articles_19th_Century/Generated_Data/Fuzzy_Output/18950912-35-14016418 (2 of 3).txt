[BY TELEGRAPH.] (FROM OUR CORRESPONDENTS.) THE BUSH FIRES AT THE KURRAJONG. GREAT DESTRUCTION OF PROPERTY. KURRAJONG, Wednesday The most fierce and disastrous bush fire that has ever been experienced in this district occurred on Monday. By about 3 o'clock the fire had ad- vanced near to Mr. Comries, of Northfield. The Heights, and was rushing on at an alarming speed with flames fully 80ft. high, which were devouring all before them. With the assistance of the neighbours and the students of Barker College the furniture was removed from the house to a clear space in front, the paling fence near was pulled down, and the roofs of the adjacent outhouses were saturated with water.
