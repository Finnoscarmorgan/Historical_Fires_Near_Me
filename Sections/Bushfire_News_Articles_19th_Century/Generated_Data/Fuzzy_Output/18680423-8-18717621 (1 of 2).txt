Thus the children have to suffer from neglect on the part of some persons. However, it is disgraceful that the matter is left in abeyance. Something must be done, or the inhabitants will at once send a memorial to the Governor upon the subject. PETTY SESSION'S AT WALLABADAH -We see a sum of money has been voted for a C.P.S. at Wallabadah. This appears a strange proceeding, and a wanton waste of the public money. BUSH-FIRE.-A person named Dogherty had his house and contents all burned by a bush-fire at Borambil Creek. April l8.
