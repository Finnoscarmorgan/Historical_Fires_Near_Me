VISIT TO THE HEYTESBURY FOREST. (From the Argus Special Reporter.) A ride through the Heytesbury forest from Camperdown to Port Campbell lies through country almost wholly devastated by the great fires of Tuesday, 5th inst. On every side farms are laid waste, houses, implements, and fencing are destroyed, and the great gumtrees and the soil are charred as black as night. The strips of country seen from the hills which caged the flames only serve to throw into relief the wide spread desolateness of the scene which the conflagration has left.
