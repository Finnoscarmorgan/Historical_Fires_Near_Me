Pastoralists between Jerilderic and Murrumbidgee are feeling the effects of the season. Reports are coming in from all sides that sheep and 6tock are dying. The water tanks are nearly exhausted, and the owners of runs are re ducing their stock as fast as possible. The weather is excessively hot. The atmosphere for the past 10 days has been thick with a smoky mist, caused by the heavy bush fires in easterly nnd northed v directions. Between here and the Murray the country still looks well, grass and water are plentiful and harvesting is in full swing. Many farmers are cutttng part of their crops for hay.
