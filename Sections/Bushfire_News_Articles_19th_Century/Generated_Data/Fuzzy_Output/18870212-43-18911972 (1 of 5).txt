More Extreme Heat. DESTRUCTIVE BUSHFIRES PREVALENT. ABOUT a week of fiercely hot weather has now been experienced, and most people are anxiously looking forward to some change. As one of the consequences of the great and long continued heat, grass, scrub, &c.;, has been rendered extremely inflammable, and fires are the order of the day. For some days past the town has been almost obscured with the smoke from the scores of fires at present raging in the forest country, south of Camperdown.
