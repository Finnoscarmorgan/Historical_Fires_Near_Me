The Bush Fires Relief Movement. MEETING OF THE DELEGATES. The first meeting of delegates appointed to supervise the distribution of the amount collected on behalf of the sufferers by the recent fires in the forest country, was held on Wednesday afternoon at the Shire Hall, Manifold street.
