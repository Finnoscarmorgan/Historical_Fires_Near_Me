MATCHES AND BUSH-FIRES. A PETITION to Parliament is now going round the country for signature, the prayer of which is that the importation into the colony of any matches other than those known as " safety matches" shall be forbidden. No one cau deny that bush fires are largely due to preventable causes, and that a very great number of them is caused by the care less use of common lucifers. Any proposal therefore by which the use of these matches can be lessened is worthy of consideration.
