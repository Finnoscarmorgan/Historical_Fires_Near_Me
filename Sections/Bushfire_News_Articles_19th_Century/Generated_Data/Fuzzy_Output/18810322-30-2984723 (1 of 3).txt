COUNTRY LETTERS (From our own correspondents.) BUNBURY, March 14th. The excessive heat referred to in my last still continues. Bush fires are destroying large quantities of timber in various localities, and one of these conflagrations almost destroyed a homestead at the Blackwood, completely des- troying a magnificent stack of hay, the out- houses and stables, belonging to Mr. Mark Padbury, in addition to two valuable brood mares, one of which was an invariable prize- winner, for years past, at our annual shows. The poor animals having been secured in the stables, and no one being at hand to rescue them, perished in the flames.
