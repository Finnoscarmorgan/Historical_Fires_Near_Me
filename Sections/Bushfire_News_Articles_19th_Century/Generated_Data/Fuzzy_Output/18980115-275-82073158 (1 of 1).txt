In the meantime Buchan managed to extricate him self froni his manacles, and having released his comrades they bound the soldiers, withdrew the flints from their firelocks and fled, taking a keg of the precious fluid with them. The soldiers managed to stagger back to the settlement and excused themselves the best way they could. Buchan, and his mates were never afterwards heard of, and it is supposed that they perished in a devouring bushfire that nearly overwhelmed the soldiers as they trotted with long faces to the set- tlement.
