AWFUL CONFLAGRATION IN PORT PHILLIP. One of the most awful fires-awful in its conse quences to life as well as property--has just taken place ,.in the beautiful district of Port Phillip. From - the accounts of the disaster which have reached us, it appears that almost at one and the same time the whole country.--was enveloped in flame. Several human creatures fell sacrifices to the devouring element, and the destruction of pro- perty was immense. Men, women, and children, were unable to avoid the progress of the fire and sank beneath its stifling influence ere it reached them.
