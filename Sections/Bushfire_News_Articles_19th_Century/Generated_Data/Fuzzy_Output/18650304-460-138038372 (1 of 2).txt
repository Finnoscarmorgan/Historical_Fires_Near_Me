KYNETON. The bush-fires that have been raging in this district during the last few days have gained in intensity day by day. Miles of bush country have had to yield to the devouring element, and in many cases farms and grass paddocks hare been one after the other de- stroyed. Between Carlsruhe and Kyneton, towards the Bald-hill, a large fire has been raging for more than a week, and on Monday and Sunday it increased in intensity, sweep ing away forms and homesteads in its relent- less path.
