The Bush Fires in Victoria. (By the Special Reporter of the Argus.) A RIDE through the Heytesbury Forest from Camperdown to Port Campbell lies through country almost wholly devastated by the great fires of Tuesday, 12th December. On every side farms are laid waste, houses, implements, and fencing are destroyed, and the great gum trees and the soil are charred as black as night.
