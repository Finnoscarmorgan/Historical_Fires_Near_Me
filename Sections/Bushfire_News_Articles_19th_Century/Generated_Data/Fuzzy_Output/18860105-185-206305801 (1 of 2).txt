SOUTH AUSTRALIA. ADELAIDE, Monday. Since the hot weather set in. numerous fires have been reported. The Venus Bay hotel, in the Elliston district has been burnt down, and bush-fires are raging in the hills. One of the fires, at Brown Hill Creek, destroyed a large amount of property; and a quantity of feed and fencing has been destroyed in an other district. The coroner is constantly re ceiving reports of small fires in dwelling houses, and cases of drowning and death from heat are also frequent.
