DISASTROUS BUSHFIRE. The most serious fire ever known in the Nagambie district occurred on Sunday It was first observed on the property of Mr W. J. Sheehan, one mile and a half from the township, and went raging through the property of Messrs Miller, Adams, M‘Donald, Newnham, Mueller, Jolly, Gorman, and Scully, and is still raging on towards the township of Avenel. Mr Mueller has lost about 1000 sheep, Mr Miller 640 acres of grass and 30 acres that were under cultiva tion, Mr M‘Donald 40 acres of crop in stooks and 300 acres of grass.
