AN EPIDEMIC OF FIRE. Widespread outbreaks in new south wales. Bush fires raging in the north. SYDNEY, Monday. Quite an epidemic of fires has raged through this colony during the past few days. Follow- ing on an extremely dry winter a hot spoil, with dry parching winds, has come to usher in spring, and with It a long train of fire disasters. On Sunday, in addition to tho great outbreaks at Wollongong, Walgett and the Scaham colliery, several bush fires occurred near various suburbs, in some cases encroaching on dwelling pieces and causing damage to fences, outhouses and verandahs.
