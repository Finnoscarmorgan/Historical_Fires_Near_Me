NEW SOUTH WALES. Sydney, March 14 The weather in some of the inland districts is extremely hot, and serious bushfires are reported in the Temora aud Orange districts, where several graziers and farmers have been completely burnt out. Great excitement has been caused at Orange by the discovery of a supposed rich silver field near the Warne Station. The country has been pegged out for miles by applicants for mineral leases. The report that the Minister of Justice is about to retire from political life and proceed to England was denied in the Assembly to-day.
