BUSH-FIRES. — On Monday several bush- fires were observed around Sandhurst — one in the direction of Break o'Day Gully, and the other towards the Whipstick. As the summer advances bush-fires will be frequent, and care should be taken by travellers not to leave fires burning in dangerous situations. — Bendigo Advertiser.
