Progress was reported at a few minutes after 12 o'clock, only one line of the second clause of the bill having been passed. On Wednesday the Wines, Beer and Spirits Sale Statute was further proceeded with, and clauses up to 17 were agreed to. Mr Francis explained wiiy the Government had not joined the San Francisco route, which was that terms were not offered which the Government could accept. On Thursday, in reply to a question, it was stated that the police would take every possi ble precaution to prevent bush-fires. Other business was transacted, the Wines, Beer and Spirits Sale Statute being advanced as far as clause 24 inclusive.
