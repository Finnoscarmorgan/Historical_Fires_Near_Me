DROUGHT AND BUSH FIRES. NEW SOUTH WALES DEVAS TATED. Sydney, September 8. The drought, accompanied by hot winds and bush fires, still continues. Some of the country townships are not yet out of danger from destruction by flames, as this afternoon a fire broke out in the shrubbery of a house at Glebe Point, within a few minutes' walk of the city. With the aid of the fire brigade it was subdued before it had a chance of spreading to any of the villas in that suburb.
