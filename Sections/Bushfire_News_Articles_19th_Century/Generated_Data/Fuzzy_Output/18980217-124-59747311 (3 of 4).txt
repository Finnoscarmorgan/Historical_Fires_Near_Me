BUSH FIRES DISASTROUS FIRE ABOVE QUEENS- TOWN. MULLERS, OF THE SAW-MILLS, BURNT OUT. ON last Tuesday week, says the Evelyn Observer, a disastrous fire occurred at the foot of the mountains, Kinglake. The day was hot and windy, making the fire all the more terrible, with the result that it burnt so fiercely that it was impossibie to do anything to stay its progress. The Muller Bros'. saw- mill was right in its course, and al though strenuous efforts were made to save the homestead and old mill, they were futile, and they were completely demolished, together with furniture, etc., the onlyarticles saved being a piano and sewing machine.
