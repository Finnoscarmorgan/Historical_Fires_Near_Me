These wattles, according to present anticipations, will be available for stripping two years hence. Putting the yield per tree at the low estimate of 10lb of bark, the total stripping will amount to 250 tons. The ruling price of wattle- bark at the present time is from £6 to £7 per ton. After the clearing at Otford was completed the lopped branches and stubble were burned, and fire- breaks have been constructed along the boundary fences to secure the plantation from bush-fire destruct- tion from the outside. The formation of an artificial wattle plantation a few miles north of Wollongong will be begun immediately after the Christ- mas holidays. By the forest depart- ment it is recognised that the growing of wattle will develop into an im- portant industry. The director general has ascertained that the southern parts of the colony are the particular habitat of the various species of wattle. M. J.
