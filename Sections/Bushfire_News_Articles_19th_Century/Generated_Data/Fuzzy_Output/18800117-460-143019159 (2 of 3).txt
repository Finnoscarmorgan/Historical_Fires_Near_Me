DESTRUCTIVE BUSH FIRE AT ROCHESTER. (ABRIDGED FROM THE BENDIGO ADVERTISES, JAN. 16.) The extensive bash fires which raged in the Rochester district on Tuesday caused serious loss to a large number of persons. Mr. P. Doherty estimates the amount of damage caused to his property at £400, whilst many other persons are losers through the fire to the extent of £190 or thereabouts. The fire appears to have originated in a paddock belonging to Mr. Macpherson, situated in Rochester West, about three miles from the township.
