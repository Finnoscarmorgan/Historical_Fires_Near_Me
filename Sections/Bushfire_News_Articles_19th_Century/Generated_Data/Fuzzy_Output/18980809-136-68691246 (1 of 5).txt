A BUSH FIRE ON CANVAS. A LOCAL PICTURE. PAINTED BY MR. LONGSTAFF. Many of our readers will remember that during the progress of the bush fires in Gippsland, Mr. John Long- staff, the celebrated Victorian Artist, paid several visits to Warragul, and saw some of the fiercest fires raging in the Bloomfield and Buln Buln East districts. His object was to obtain material for a realistic picture, and he was singularly fortunate in his mission.
