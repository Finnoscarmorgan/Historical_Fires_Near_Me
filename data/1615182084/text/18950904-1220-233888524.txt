Macleay Progress Committee. The Macleay Progress Committee met on Monday night m Mr. John Camp bell's auction mart. Mr. P. C. Hill occupied the chair and about a score of members attended. The secretary (Mr. Williams) reported the enrol ment of eight new members. The chairman stated that the sub committee appointed at the previous meeting had waited on the Mayor, asking him to head the deputation to Sydney to wait on the Minister for Works. The Mayor, however, was unable to accept the invitation, so the sub-committee had arranged for Mr. O. O. Dangar to go. The meeting by resolution endorsed the action of the sub-committee.
