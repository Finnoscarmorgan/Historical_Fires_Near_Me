THE COUNTRY. FIRE AT QUEENSCLIFFE. TWO HUNDRED SHEEP BURNT. QUEENSCLIFFE, January 6.-A serious bush- <*> fire started on Sunday near Mr. Ward's property, about 12 miles from Queenscliffe. The grass and trees burnt fiercely, the flames miles of the township. About 1,000 acres of grass were burnt and 200 sheep, the owners of which are not known. A lot of fencing was also destroyed and other damage was done. By timely aid much damage was prevented. The fire^ still burning, but owing to the cool change further damage is not anticipated. An inquest will be held.
