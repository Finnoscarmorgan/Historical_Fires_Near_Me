CORRESPONDENCE. BUSH FIRES RELIEF FUND. TO TItE EDITOR OF THE OIIRONICLE. SIR,-His Worship the Mayor, ,eing assured that the generous feelings which are now being exhibited in Melbourne and elsewhere towards the sufferers by the bush fires in Gippsland also exist in Williams town, desires to give the residents an opportunity of subscribing to the fund now being raised for their relief. Lists are circulated in the town, which are expected to be returned by the 10th Febuary next. Yours truly, GEO. F. SMITH, Town Clerk. [Subscriptions received at this o6flce.-ED. Chron.] -
