BUSHFIRES IN NEW SOUTH WALES. [By Telegraph.] Sydney, November 16. Bushfires are reported to be raging in the Nevertire, Forbes, Orange, Bathurst, Newcastle, and many other districts of the colony. An immense amount of feed and fencing has already been destroyed. Great damage was done at Avoca-park, near Smlthfield, yesterday, the house of Mlsb English being destroyed, along with an empty house and several outhouses, while several other residences were only saved by the strenuous efforts of the occu piers.
