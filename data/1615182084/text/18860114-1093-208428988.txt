NEW ZEALAND. [From our own Correspondent.] Auckland. January 13. The Premier denies a rumor which has gained currency that Mr. Larnarch is to be appointed to the Agent-Generalship. He says that the Government have no intention of replacing Sir F. Dillon Bell. Kain has fallen in the south, and has effectually extinguished the bushfires that were raging so fiercely.
