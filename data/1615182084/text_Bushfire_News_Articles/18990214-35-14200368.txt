BUSHFIRE NEAR COROWA COROWA, Monday. Mr. Smith, of Sunbury, lost 300 acres of splendid grass and fencing by a fire which started on Satur- day and continued all Sunday. It is still burning. Fortunately a creek prevents it spreading to the ad- joining paddock, which is part of Quat Quatta station. The paddock was to be let for starving stock from Urana district on the 16 th proximo.
