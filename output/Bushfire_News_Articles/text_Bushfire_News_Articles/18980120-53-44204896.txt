THE GIPPSLAND FIRES. Still Burning. [By Telegraph] MELBOURNE. Thursday Afternoon. Rush fires are still raging in the vicinity of Warragul, in South Gippsland, yester- day several fine homesteads were destroyed, also a large quantity of hay and grass. Fires are also threatening Neerim, Inverloch, and Leongatha. A large fire is also burning near Maffra. The Mayor's fund for the relief of the suffering now amounts to £1700 end the Argus fund to £2200.
