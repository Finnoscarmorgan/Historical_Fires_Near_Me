Two suicides took place in the suburbs this morning. A married woman named Marlon Jane Potts, residing at Summer Hill, hanged herself in a bathroom. She had been in bad health for several months and had made two previous. attempts at suicide. The second case was that of a man named Stephen Morris, a laborer, whose body, with a shot wound in the head and a revolver lying by the side, wa3 found in a paddock off Point street.
