The Weather. The smoky atmosphere which, enveloped this district on Sunday last, and to a less, extent on Monday, caused some speculation as to its origin. As there were not any bush fires in this colony. it was surmised that the smoke had been carried by southerly winds, from those terribly destructive bush.'fires' in ."Victoria, and this is probably correct. The weather here has not been unsea- sonably warm of late, but has been generalty speaking pleasant and enjoyable.
