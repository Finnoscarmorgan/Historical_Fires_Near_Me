TASMANIA. THE SUPPOSED VOLCANO. HOBABT. Sunday. The reported volcanic eruption at the Snug River has caused a large amount of interest. Testerday and to-day hundreds went to the locality by sjeamers, and numerous parties by land, but nearly all failed in reaching the conflagration. They brought bask most con tradictory suppositions as to the cause, only agreeing as to the density and blackness of -the smoke. The special reporter of the Mercury says that it is an immense bush-fire which has seized the peat, moss, and button grass.
