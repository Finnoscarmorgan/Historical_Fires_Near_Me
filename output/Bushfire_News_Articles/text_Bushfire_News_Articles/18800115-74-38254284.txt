SOUTH AUSTRALIA. ADELAIDE, Jan. 9. At a meeting of the stewards of the Newmarket meeting this afternoon, a re solution was passed warning J. E. Saville, of Morphetville, off the course at New market meetings for the space of twelve months, for using abusive language to Mr Crozier, one of the stewards. The perfect skeleton of a blackfellow has been found in an excavation for sewerage works near the Adelaide railway station. It has been sent to the museum. Severe bushfires are occurring in various parts of the country. Jan. 12. Wheat is steady at 4s lid.
